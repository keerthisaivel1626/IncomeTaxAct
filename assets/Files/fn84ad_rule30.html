84ad. Sub-rule (5) substituted by the Income-tax (Eleventh Amendment) Rules, 2016, w.e.f. 1-6-2016. Prior to its substitution, said sub-rule read as under :

"(5) For the purpose of sub-rule (4), the Director General Income-tax (Systems) shall specify the procedures, formats and standards for ensuring secure capture and transmission of data, and shall also be responsible for the day-to-day administration in relation to furnishing the information in the manner so specified."
