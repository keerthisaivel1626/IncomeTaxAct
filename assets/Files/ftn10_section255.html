10. Sub. for "fifteen lakh rupees" by Act No. 28 of 2016 (w.e.f. 1-6-2016). Earlier "fifteen lakh rupees" was substituted for "five hundred thousand rupees" by the Finance Act, 2015, w.e.f. 1-6-2015, "five hundred thousand rupees" was substituted for "one hundred thousand rupees" by the Finance (No. 2) Act, 1998, w.e.f. 1-10-1998, "one hundred thousand rupees" was substituted for "forty thousand rupees" by the Direct Tax Laws (Amendment) Act, 1989, w.e.f. 1-4-1989, "forty thousand rupees" was substituted for "twenty-five thousand rupees" by the Taxation Laws (Amendment) Act, 1970, w.e.f. 1-4-1971.
