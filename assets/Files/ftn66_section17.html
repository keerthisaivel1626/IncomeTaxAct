66. In terms of section 10A of the Salaries and Allowances of Ministers Act, 1952/Salaries and Allowances of Officers of Parliament Act, 1953 and section 9A of the Salary and Allowances of Leaders of Opposition in Parliament Act, 1977, value of rent-free furnished residence (including maintenance thereof) provided to a minister/an officer of Parliament and a Leader of the Opposition is not to be included in the computation of his income chargeable to tax under the head "Salaries".
