(a) Specialised boilers and furnaces :

  (i) Economisers and feed water heaters

 (ii) Recuperators and air pre-heaters

(iii) Back pressure turbines for co-generation

(iv) Heat pumps

 (v) Vapour absorption refrigeration system

(vi) Organic rankine cycle power system

(vii) Low inlet pressure small steam turbines

(d) Power factor correcting devices:

(i) Shunt capacitors and synchronous condenser systems.
