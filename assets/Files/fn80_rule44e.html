80. Sub-rule (1) substituted by the IT (Twelfth Amendment) Rules, 2014, w.e.f. 28-11-2014. Prior to its substitution, said sub-rule, as amended by the IT (Tenth Amendment) Rules, 1999, w.e.f. 28-5-1999; IT (Thirteenth Amendment) Rules, 2000, w.e.f. 3-8-2000 and IT (Eighteenth Amendment) Rules, 2013, w.e.f. 1-4-2015, read as under :

(1) An application for obtaining an advance ruling under sub-section (1) of section 245Q shall be made in quadruplicate,—

(a)  in Form No. 34C in respect of a non-resident applicant;
(b)  in Form No. 34D in respect of a resident applicant seeking advance ruling in relation to a transaction undertaken or proposed to be undertaken by him with a non-resident; and
(c)  in Form No. 34E in respect of a resident falling within any such class or category of person as notified by the Central Government in exercise of the powers conferred by sub-clause (iii) of clause (b) of section 245N,
  and shall be verified in the manner indicated therein.
(d)  in Form No. 34EA, in respect of an applicant referred to in sub-clause (iiia) of clause (b) of section 245N of the Act."
